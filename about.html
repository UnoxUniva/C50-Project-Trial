About us page

This page is a page which is about the creators of this webpage. WhiteHat Jr is an Indian ed-tech startup and online tutoring firm that teaches coding to kids via one-to-one live video classes. It was founded in 2018 by Karan Bajaj and is targeted at children aged 6 years to 14 years in countries like India and the U.S. In 2020, WhiteHat Jr was acquired by BYJU'S for USD$300 million. It also claims to offer AI courses to its customers.
